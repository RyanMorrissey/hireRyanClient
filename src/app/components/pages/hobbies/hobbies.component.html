Model Painting and 3D Printing

During covid, one of the hobbies I picked up was model painting. What started as a way to get into a tabletop game became a nice creative outlet. I started to buy more paints, then more models, then even more paints, and all of a sudden I had a huge supply of materials. Painting became more fun than playing, so I focused more of my time on that.

I then started to look into 3D printers as a way to get cheaper models, but from there discovered resin statues and larger builds. 3D printing props, statues, and terrain became more fun than collecting models. I could work on different techniques and create some unique gifts for friends and family.
